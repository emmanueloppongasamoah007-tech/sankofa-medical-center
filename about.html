Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

The principles that guide every decision we make and every patient we care for.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide accessible, compassionate, and evidence-based healthcare to every patient who walks through our doors, regardless of background, and using the most advanced medical technologies and a team of dedicated professionals.

We are committed to treating not just the illness, but the whole person: mind, body, and spirit.

🌟

Our Vision

To be the leading centre of medical excellence in West Africa, a hospital where innovation meets compassion, where every staff member is empowered to deliver outstanding care, and where every patient leaves healthier, happier, and more informed.

We aspire to set the regional benchmark for patient outcomes and healthcare delivery.

Our Journey

Our History

Two decades of growth, learning, and unwavering commitment to our community.

2005

Foundation

Sankofa Medical Center was established with a team of 12 doctors and a vision to make quality healthcare accessible to all.

2009

Cardiology Wing Opens

Expanded services with the launch of a dedicated cardiology unit, equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic tools.

2013

National Accreditation

Received full national accreditation from the Ghana Health Authority, recognising our standards of clinical excellence.

2017

New Pediatrics Centre

Opened a child-friendly dedicated paediatric wing with specialist neonatal care.

2022

Digital Transformation

Launched our online appointment booking system, reducing wait times and improving patient access.

2025

15,000 Patients Milestone

Celebrated treating over 15,000 patients and expanded to 50+ specialist doctors across 6 departments.
