Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How do I book an appointment?

Use our online booking form to select your department, preferred date, and time. You'll receive a reference number on confirmation.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes. Please call our main line at +233 25 659 4485 at least 24 hours before your appointment to cancel or reschedule.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card (if applicable), and any existing medical records or prescriptions.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes. Our Emergency Unit is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Call +233 25 659 4485 or come directly to our Emergency entrance.

Are there facilities for children?

Absolutely. Our dedicated Paediatrics wing is designed to be child-friendly, with specialist staff trained in paediatric care.

Do you accept health insurance?

We accept most major national health insurance schemes. Please contact us in advance to confirm your specific coverage.

Still have questions? Don't hesitate to reach out.
